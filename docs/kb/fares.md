# MBTA Fares and Payment

## Subway and bus fares
The standard subway fare is $2.40 and the standard local bus fare is $1.70 when paying with a
CharlieCard. Paying with cash or a contactless card (CharlieTicket) on the bus costs the same
$1.70, but the subway charge is $2.40. Children 11 and under ride free when accompanied by a
fare-paying adult (up to two children per adult).

## Free and reduced transfers
With a CharlieCard you get free or reduced transfers within two hours of your first tap: subway
to bus, bus to subway, and bus to bus are included in one fare. This is why tapping your
CharlieCard (rather than paying cash) is usually cheaper for trips with a connection.

## Passes
The 7-Day and Monthly LinkPass provide unlimited travel on the subway and local bus. The Monthly
LinkPass is $90 and is best for riders who commute most days. Commuter Rail and ferry have their
own zone-based monthly passes, which also include subway and bus access.

## Commuter Rail and ferry
Commuter Rail fares are zone-based: the farther the zone from downtown Boston, the higher the
fare. Ferry fares depend on the route. CharlieCards are not used for Commuter Rail zones; fares
are bought via the mTicket app or onboard.

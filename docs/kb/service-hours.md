# MBTA Service Hours and Frequency

## Span of service
The subway and most bus routes run from roughly 5:00 AM until about 1:00 AM. The first trains
leave terminal stations around 5:00–5:30 AM and the last trains depart downtown around 12:30 AM.
There is no overnight subway service; late-night travel relies on a limited set of bus routes and
ride-hail.

## Frequency
During weekday rush hours the Red, Orange, and Blue Lines run every few minutes. Off-peak and on
weekends, trains run less frequently, often every 8–12 minutes. The Green Line frequency varies by
branch (B, C, D, E). Bus frequency varies widely by route; key bus routes run every 10–15 minutes.

## Why a train might not be running now
Outside the span of service (after about 1 AM and before 5 AM) there are simply no scheduled
trains. During the day, a gap can be caused by disabled trains, signal problems, single-tracking
for maintenance, or weather. These disruptions are published as real-time service alerts.

## Holidays and weekends
The MBTA runs a reduced "Saturday" or "Sunday" schedule on major holidays. Weekend schedules have
longer headways than weekday schedules, and some express bus routes do not run on weekends.
